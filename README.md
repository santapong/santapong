## About me

Software engineer at **Ennovie**, a jewelry manufacturing company in Thailand. I work on the **Digital Platforms & Automation** team — building AI agents, production scheduling systems, and data pipelines.

I maintain a small Kubernetes cluster at home (three mini PCs in a closet), and I'm working toward a **Master's in Quantum Computing** by 2027.

> *cheap + best = ship it 🚀 · expensive + marginal = pass ✋*
